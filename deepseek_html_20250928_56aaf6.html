How to Use the Particle Sandbox

  • Add Particles: Click and drag on the canvas to add particles
  • Add Random: Click the "Add Random" button to add particles with random velocities
  • Gravity: Toggle gravity on/off and adjust its strength
  • Friction: Adjust how quickly particles slow down
  • Trails: Enable trails to see particle paths
  • Interact: Click and drag existing particles to move them
